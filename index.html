    Who is Harry_Potter_

    Harry Potter was a young wizard with bright emerald green eyes that twinkled like stars in the night sky and hair as black as coal. He was a brave boy who wasn't afraid to stand up for his beliefs and fight for his friends. His courage often led him into difficult situations, but he never gave up without a fight. Harry taught us to never give up. He destroyed the horcrux within himself, and then came back from the dead to kill Voldemort. Harry endured so much in the series, and he persevered through all of it. He showed us that you can push through even the hardest times, bounce back and succeed. The first Harry Potter book, Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, was published in 1997 to immediate popular and critical acclaim. Six further best-selling books and eight blockbuster films followed.
